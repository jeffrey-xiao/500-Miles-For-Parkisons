Toronto Event

Join us on Wednesday June 22, 2016 at Steam Whistle Brewing’s historic Roundhouse, to celebrate the completion of 500 Miles by our courageous walkers: Harry McMurtry, Sue Thompson and Ross Sugar. The evening will be a celebration of their journey from New York City to Toronto to raise money and awareness for Parkinson’s. The evening will feature surprises, special guests, plenty of food and libations, and old friends.

The Roundhouse, is located at 255 Bremner Blvd., Toronto, ON, M5V 3M9. The party will be from 7:00 p.m. to 2:00 a.m.
